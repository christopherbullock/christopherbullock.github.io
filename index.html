North Carolina Red Wolf Habitat Map

Check out the project!

This was the final result of my MAP671 class! I had decided on my interest in wildlife and projects to re-establish red wolves to North America, but specifically the state I currently live in, North Carolina. As such, I used census data of areas identified as "natural" and acreage requirements for red wolf populations to create this map.
